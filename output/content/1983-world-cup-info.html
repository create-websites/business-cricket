THE DAY THE SCRIPT WAS TORN APART

On June 9, 1983, two seismic shocks in Group B blew the tournament wide open, proving no one was invincible.

UPSET 1: THE GIANT SLAYERS

India vs. West Indies

India achieved the impossible, handing the West Indies their first-ever World Cup defeat. Yashpal Sharma's 89 anchored a 34-run victory.

UPSET 2: THE DEBUTANT'S DREAM

Zimbabwe vs. Australia

In their first ever ODI, part-timers from Zimbabwe stunned Australia, with captain Duncan Fletcher leading them to a historic 13-run win.

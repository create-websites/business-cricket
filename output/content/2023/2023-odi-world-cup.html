The Record Breakers

Virat Kohli: The Run Machine

With 765 runs, Kohli shattered the 20-year-old record for most runs in a single World Cup and became the first player to score 50 ODI centuries.

Despite missing the first four games, Shami finished as the tournament's top wicket-taker with 24 scalps, including a record-breaking 7/57 in the semi-final.
